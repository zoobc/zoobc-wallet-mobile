Overview

ZooBC wallet is a mobile application that is able to connect with the ZooBC blockchain network, so it can carry out activities such as creating accounts, viewing balances, sending coins, displaying QRcode addresses.

Dashboard

In the application dashboard you can see the total balance of all accounts that you have created in this application.

Receive

Will have QRCode of your account address. Your friend can scan qrcode and then send a number of coins to you.

Send

The send button for sending coins from your account to your other account or to someone else's account, you need a destination coin address.
